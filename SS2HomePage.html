About Us

Token Impact is an interactive fundraising initiative designed to uplift small rural businesses in South Africa. By combining gamification, ad revenue, and community engagement, we create a fun and impactful way for people of all ages to get involved. Our games reward players with tokens that can be used to support the local rural businesses of South Africa which in return it is building a sustainable cycle of growth and opportunity. Wwe aim to empower communities, one token at a time.
